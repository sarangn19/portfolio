Problem

Gifting platforms that feel like everything else

Generic — same products everywhere
Emotionally disconnected from the occasion
Hard to explore by occasion or intent
Overwhelming product listings with no hierarchy

Users don’t just want products — they want meaningful gifting experiences.

Goal

Premium, culturally-rooted, and intentional

Emotional & intentional
Make gifting feel meaningful, not transactional.
Simplified discovery
Help users find the right gift without friction.
Luxury + trust
Build a premium perception grounded in cultural authenticity.
Target Users

Who we designed for

Primary Segment
Young professionals, 22–35
Buying gifts for weddings, festivals (Diwali, Eid, Bhai Dooj), and corporate occasions.
What they value
Aesthetics · Cultural authenticity · Premium feel
They want the gift to say something — not just fill a box.
Key Insight

Users search for moments, not products

Users don’t search for “products.” They search for moments and occasions. The navigation had to reflect that.

Solution

Six decisions that shaped the experience

01
Occasion-first navigation
Wedding / Eid / Diwali / Corporate — users start with intent, not filters. Reduces cognitive load before they even see a product.
02
Emotion-driven hero section
Strong visual storytelling with warm colors and premium product imagery. Creates instant brand positioning on first scroll.
03
Clean product discovery
Card-based layout, minimal clutter, clear pricing hierarchy. Faster scanning and better decisions.
04
Premium visual language
Earthy tones (brown, beige, gold), soft shadows, rounded cards, cultural textures. Feels luxury, not mass-market.
05
Trust-building features
“Best sellers,” “Only a few left,” clear pricing and discounts. Improves conversion confidence without pressure tactics.
06
Feature storytelling section
“Why Sandookchi” highlights customisation and reinforces brand value. Not just selling — explaining value.
