Pulse — Creative Agency Website

Designed a modern agency website focused on building trust, communicating value clearly, and converting visitors into inquiries.

Type UI Exploration
Focus Landing Page · Conversion Design
Tools Figma · Layout Systems

Pulse — full page layout

Context

A UI exploration for a creative agency

This project was aimed at designing a clean, high-conversion website for a creative agency. The goal was to balance visual appeal with clarity — ensuring users quickly understand what the agency does and feel confident reaching out.

Core Focus

Three goals driving every decision

⚡
Instant clarity — what the agency does, communicated within seconds of landing.
🤝
Trust building — through work samples, client logos, and testimonials placed strategically.
📩
Lead conversion — clear, accessible contact points that appear after trust is established.
Key Design Decisions

Where the thinking shows

01 — Hero
Strong, benefit-driven headline
"Less Ordinary. More Wow." — bold and memorable, paired with a visual that communicates creativity instantly.
02 — Flow
Structured storytelling from top to bottom
Who we are → What we do → Proof → People → Clients → Testimonials → Contact. This reduces cognitive load and builds trust progressively.
03 — Services
Modular, scannable service cards
Services are broken into clear blocks so users understand offerings at a glance — no long paragraphs to wade through.
04 — Credibility
Work showcase for instant proof
Visual project previews let users evaluate the agency's capability and style quickly, before reading a single word.
05 — Personality
Humanizing the brand
A founder section adds relatability and personality, making the agency feel approachable rather than corporate.
06 — Social Proof
Clients + testimonials before the CTA
Social proof is placed strategically just before the contact section — reinforcing credibility at the moment of decision.
07 — Conversion
Low-friction contact form
Simple, minimal form placed after trust is fully established — increasing the likelihood that visitors actually reach out.
Visual & UI Thinking

Design choices that support the goals

Clean layout with generous spacing to improve readability and reduce visual noise.
Limited colour palette to maintain brand consistency throughout the page.
Subtle gradients used sparingly for a modern, polished feel.
Clear typography hierarchy so users can scan and find what they need fast.
Learnings

What this project taught me

Content flow is everything on landing pages — the order of sections directly affects how much trust a visitor builds.
Trust is built through multiple small signals, not one big statement. Each section adds a layer.
Clarity and structure outperform visual complexity every time in high-conversion design.
