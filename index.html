About Me

Click to add photo

I'm Sarang N, a B.Design (UI/UX) student at Lovely Professional University (CGPA 8.19), with a growing focus on product design and end-to-end UX.

My background spans graphic design, brand identity, and UI/UX — giving me a holistic view of how design communicates at every touchpoint. I've worked with startups remotely, designing marketing assets, brand systems, and user interfaces.

Currently interning as a UI/UX Designer at Last Minute Deal, where I'm building design systems, conducting research, and creating wireframes for real product flows. My personal case studies reflect my passion for complex, meaningful problem spaces.
